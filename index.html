Empowering Nigeria’s Future Leaders Through Education

The National Tertiary Education Fund (NTEF) Scholarship is your gateway to academic success! Whether you’re an undergraduate, polytechnic student, or pursuing a master’s or PhD, this scholarship is designed to support your educational journey.

Simple Steps to Secure Your NTEF Scholarship

1

Register on the NTEF Application Platform by clicking the "APPLY NOW" button to get started.

2

Proceed to the payment gateway. Only pay to NTEF’s recommended account.

3

Your application will be reviewed. After successful evaluation, you’ll receive feedback via email. If approved, payments will be made to your bank or institution.

Scholarship Benefits

Undergraduate Students

Successful undergraduate recipients will receive a ₦300,000 scholarship, disbursed as:

  • One-time or two-time payment directly into the student’s verified bank account.
  • Can be used for tuition, learning materials, and living expenses.
  • Includes access to NTEF career support, mentorship programs, and exclusive development events.

Postgraduate Students (MSc)

Successful MSc recipients will receive a ₦500,000 scholarship, disbursed as:

  • One-time or two-time payment depending on the program duration and verification status.
  • Covers tuition fees, research/project support, and academic-related costs.
  • Includes access to development-focused programs, industry mentorship, and internship opportunities.

Postgraduate Students (PhD)

PhD awardees will receive an advanced support package (amount to be specified), disbursed as:

  • Two-time disbursement to align with research phases and progress milestones.
  • Covers research funding, conference travel, publication assistance, and academic expenses.
  • Priority access to global research networks, national think tanks, and policy-impact initiatives.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Be Nigerian citizens.
  • Be enrolled in a recognized Nigerian polytechnic (ND/HND) or university (undergraduate or postgraduate).
  • Meet ONE of the following academic requirements:
    • Fresh Students (no GPA/CGPA): JAMB score of 180 or above.
    • Continuing Students: Minimum GPA/CGPA of 2.5 on a 4.0 scale (or equivalent).
  • Commit to full-time study during the scholarship period.

General Eligibility (All Students)

Applicants must:

  • Be a Nigerian citizen with a valid National Identity Number (NIN).
  • Be enrolled or completed a tertiary institution (university, polytechnic, or college of education) in Nigeria.
  • Have a minimum of 5 O'Level credits including English Language and Mathematics.
  • Provide accurate and verifiable documentation.

Postgraduate Applicants – MSc

  • Must possess a minimum of Second Class Upper (2.1) in their first degree, or Second Class Lower (2.2) with relevant industry experience.
  • Must have completed the National Youth Service Corps (NYSC).
  • Be computer literate.
  • Submit a Statement of Purpose (max 500 words) stating:
    • The reason for the proposed study
    • Its relevance to the industry
    • Its expected impact on national development

Postgraduate Applicants – PhD

  • Must have completed the National Youth Service Corps (NYSC).
  • Be computer literate.
  • Possess a minimum of Second Class Lower (2.2) in their first degree and a good Master's degree.
  • Research must be relevant to national development goals.

*Note: Postgraduate applicants will be reached out to separately via email provided.
