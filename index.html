Empowering Nigeria’s Future Leaders Through Education

The National Tertiary Education Fund (NTEF) Scholarship is your gateway to academic success! Whether you’re an undergraduate, polytechnic student, or pursuing a master’s or PhD, this scholarship is designed to support your educational journey.

Simple Steps to Secure Your NTEF Scholarship

1

Register on the NTEF Application Platform by clicking the "APPLY NOW" button to get started.

2

Proceed to the payment gateway. Only pay to NTEF’s recommended account.

3

Your application will be reviewed. After successful evaluation, you’ll receive feedback via email. If approved, payments will be made to your bank or institution.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 1. Be Nigerian citizens.
  • 2. Be enrolled in a recognized Nigerian polytechnic (ND/HND) or university (undergraduate or postgraduate).
  • 3. Meet ONE of the following academic requirements:
    • Fresh Students (no GPA/CGPA): JAMB score of 180 or above.
    • Continuing Students: Minimum GPA/CGPA of 2.5 on a 4.0 scale (or equivalent).
  • 4. Commit to full-time study during the scholarship period.

Reward System

Top 100 Students:

  • Branded laptop + academic backpack
  • N250,000 annual stipend (renewable yearly, contingent on maintaining a GPA/CGPA ≥ 2.5)

All Qualifying Candidates:

  • N250,000 annual stipend (renewable yearly, subject to sustained academic performance)

Total Recipients: Scholarship is limited to only 100,000 students nationwide (including the top 100).
